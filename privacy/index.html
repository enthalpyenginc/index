Privacy Policy

Last updated: —

1. Who we are

Enthalpy Engineering Incorporated (“Enthalpy”, “we”, “us”) operates this website at enthalpyinc.com.

2. What information we collect

  • Information you provide: If you submit an inquiry form, we collect the information you enter (such as your name, email address, organization, and message).
  • Analytics data (optional): If you consent to analytics cookies, we collect usage information about how visitors interact with the site (for example pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser information). This data is used to improve the website.

3. How we use information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about projects or services.
  • Improve our website and understand site usage (only if you consent to analytics).

4. Cookies and analytics

We store a consent preference in your browser (local storage) to remember whether you accepted or rejected optional analytics. If you accept analytics, our site enables Firebase Analytics (Google). If you reject, analytics is not loaded.

You can change your choice anytime using the “Cookie settings” link in the website footer.

5. Third-party service providers

We use third parties to operate certain site functions:

  • Form submission processor: Our contact form is delivered using FormSubmit. When you submit a form, the information you enter is transmitted to FormSubmit to deliver the message to our email inbox.
  • Analytics: If you consent, we use Firebase Analytics (Google) to measure and understand website usage.

6. Data retention

Inquiry messages are retained as long as needed to respond and for reasonable business records. Analytics data is retained according to the settings in Firebase/Google.

7. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers described above and when required by law.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Your choices

  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your inquiry information where applicable.
  • You can opt out of optional analytics via Cookie settings.

10. Contact
